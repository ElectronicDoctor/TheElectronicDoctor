About Electronic Doctor

Electronic Doctor is a locally-owned tech repair business built on trust, honesty, and fast turnaround times. We repair phones, laptops, gaming consoles, tablets, and other electronics with care and precision.

Our mission is simple: provide high-quality, affordable repairs so you don’t have to replace your device. We believe in transparent pricing, reliable service, and treating every customer like family.

Whether it’s a cracked screen, slow computer, overheating console, or general troubleshooting — we’ve got you covered.
